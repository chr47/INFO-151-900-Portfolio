ASSOCIATIONS

Philly Codefest

Drexel University’s annual hackathon that brings together students and professionals to collaborate on creative software and hardware projects. It’s a great opportunity to develop skills, network, and innovate in a supportive environment.

Hack4Impact At Drexel

A student-run chapter at Drexel University of the nationwide nonprofit Hack4Impact, focused on building custom software solutions for local nonprofits in the Philadelphia region. Members gain real-world development experience, project management, and teamwork skills while contributing to social good.

Drexel Dragon Climbers

A student-led climbing club that promotes fitness, skill-building, and community among Drexel students. The club organizes regular indoor and outdoor climbing sessions, workshops, and social events, welcoming climbers of all experience levels.

Association for Computer Machinery

The ACM is a leading professional organization for computing professionals and students. It offers resources like conferences, publications, and networking opportunities to help members stay updated on the latest in computer science and technology.
